    How We Determine Popularity

    Our methodology combines multiple sources:
    • TIOBE Index: Based on search engine queries (Google, Bing, Yahoo)
    • Stack Overflow Survey: Annual developer survey with 90,000+ responses
    • GitHub Octoverse: Analysis of repository languages and contributions
    • PYPL Index: Google Trends analysis of language tutorial searches

    Note: Rankings can vary by source and year. Python has consistently held the top spot since 2021 due to AI/ML growth.
